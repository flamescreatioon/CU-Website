About Our Alumni Association

Rooted in Christ. Connected as Family. Driven by Purpose

Our Rich History

From humble beginnings to a global network of accomplished Eagles

Founding of Central High School

1948

Central High School opened its doors for the first time, welcoming students with a commitment to academic excellence and character development.

First Graduating Class

1952

The pioneering class of Eagles graduated, establishing traditions that would define our school for generations to come.

Alumni Association Formed

1975

A dedicated group of graduates officially established the Alumni Association to maintain connections and support the school's growth.

First Alumni Scholarship

1987

The association awarded its first scholarship to a deserving senior, beginning a tradition of alumni supporting the next generation.

25th Anniversary Celebration

2000

The largest homecoming in school history brought together over 2,000 alumni to celebrate a quarter-century of the association.

Launch of STEM Initiative

2023

The Alumni Association announced its ambitious plan to fund the STEM Innovation Center, ushering in a new era of alumni impact.

Our Mission & Values

The Federal Government College Warri Christian Union Alumni is a growing network of former students of the Great college who identify as christians, and identifies with the Christian Union body. It is a community open to past members of the fellowship or even just general alumni of the school as long as you believe in Jesus Christ and our shared mission to give back light, hope and guidance to the current students, the fellowship and the school. We were shaped not just by books and classrooms, but by the spirit of Christ-centered values that defined our high school experience. This is more than a group — it’s a spiritual family.

Our Core Values:

1Christ Centres

We put God at the center of every gathering, decision, and project.

2Unity in Diversity

Though we’ve walked different paths, our roots keep us one.

3Service to Legacy

We believe in giving back — with time, talent, and treasure.

4Love and Family

Every member is family, we nurture one another..
