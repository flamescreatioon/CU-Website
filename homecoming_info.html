Homecoming 2025: A Time to Reconnect

This is the moment we’ve all been waiting for — the official Federal Government College Warri Christian Union Alumni Homecoming 2025 is here! Let’s relive the memories, strengthen old bonds, and ignite new impact through fellowship and purpose-driven action.

Event Details

  • Date: June 27th - 29th, 2025
  • Time: Friday 12PM; Saturday & Sunday: 9AM
  • Venue: Federal Government College Warri
  • Note: Bring your energy and another Alumni!
