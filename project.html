Our Project: Building a Legacy of Impact

Because our alma mater gave us more than an education — it gave us purpose.

Why We're Giving Back

As alumni of CU body, we carry more than just memories — we carry a responsibility to uplift those coming behind us. Our collective growth in faith, leadership, and purpose was shaped by this school. Now, we’re choosing to sow back into that same ground — and you’re a key part of it.

This year, as part of our Alumni Homecoming 2025, we’re launching a transformative project that will leave a lasting legacy.

The Vision: A standard worship center

Project Goal: Worship Center

Impact Highlights:

  • Gives the students a permanent worship house, unaffected by events or activities
  • Supports the school’s long-term growth and development plan
  • A safe space managed by the CU Body to keep the fellowship’s equipment and properties, reducing movement and prolonging their lifespan
  • Unites alumni around a common mission of legacy and love
